Privacy Policy

Plant Doctor · Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Plant Doctor ("we", "our", "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information. Plant Doctor is developed by Phoenixbit Studio. By using the app, you agree to the practices described here.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide plant identification and diagnosis features using AI.
  • To let you save, sync, and back up your garden, journal, and wishlist data.
  • To verify purchases and unlock premium features you've paid for.
  • To diagnose and fix crashes or errors in the app.
  • To send optional local reminders (e.g., watering schedules) — these stay on your device and are not sent to us.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your account data and usage records are stored on Supabase (our backend provider) with encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS). Plant photos are processed for AI analysis and are not permanently stored on our servers beyond what's needed to return a result. Garden/journal/wishlist data is stored locally on your device by default, and only leaves your device if you explicitly enable Google Drive backup — in that case, it is stored in your own Google Drive account, which we cannot access.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Gemini (AI) — processes photos you submit to identify or diagnose plants.
  • Google Sign-In / Google Drive — optional, used only if you choose to sign in with Google or enable backup.
  • Supabase — our backend provider for authentication and data storage.
  • Sentry — crash and error reporting to help us improve app stability.
  • Google Play Billing — processes in-app purchases and subscriptions.

5. Your Choices

  • Location tagging is optional and can be skipped for any plant entry.
  • Google Drive backup is optional and can be turned off at any time in Settings.
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

Plant Doctor is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
