Delete Your Account

Plant Doctor

You can request deletion of your Plant Doctor account and all associated data at any time.

How to request deletion

Send an email to infinitedreams.chls@gmail.com with:

  • Subject: "Delete my account"
  • The email address you used to sign in to Plant Doctor (Google Sign-In)

We will process your request and confirm by email once it's completed, within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login information (Supabase Authentication)
  • Your garden, journal, and wishlist data stored on our servers
  • Your subscription/purchase records

What may be retained

  • Data you backed up to your own Google Drive is stored in your Google account, not ours — you can delete it yourself from Google Drive at any time.
  • Anonymized crash/error logs (Sentry) that do not identify you personally may be retained for debugging purposes.
  • Records we are legally required to keep (e.g., purchase records for tax/accounting purposes) may be retained as required by law.

Delete data without deleting your account

Currently, we only support full account deletion. If you'd like specific data removed while keeping your account, mention this in your email and we'll assist on a case-by-case basis.
